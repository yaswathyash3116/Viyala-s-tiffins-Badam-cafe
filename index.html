28 Years of Tradition

Born from the Heart of
Andhra Pradesh

Born from the rich culinary traditions of Andhra Pradesh, our journey began with a simple yet timeless recipe — the beloved Badam Milk. What started as a humble family tradition has grown into a soulful experience, bringing the authentic taste of Andhra to every cup we serve.

Passed down through generations, our Badam Milk is crafted with care, patience, and pride. We use premium almonds, slow-boiled milk, and a perfect blend of handpicked spices to create a drink that is both refreshing and nourishing.

Every sip tells a story — of heritage, warmth, and the unmistakable flavors of Andhra. It's not just a drink, it's a tradition we are proud to share.
